Windows 10 has a function called Cortana which is a voice-controlled assistant. This assistant makes it easier to communicate with your device by talking to it. Some features are; you can search your hard drive for specific files, find photos, and launch apps downloaded on your computer/device.
Windows 10 also has a function called Multiple Desktops which allows you to run multiple sets of screens. This makes it easier to manage your windows and apps. You can also run other apps in the background while browsing the web for easy multi-tasking. 
Another function of Windows 10 OS is the Xbox app. This feature allows you to play any Xbox One game on your device. This function supports streaming games through your network and performs with improved speed and graphics. You can also record and share your gameplay.
